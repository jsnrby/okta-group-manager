# Okta Group Manager

Welcome! This assistant lets Okta group owners manage their groups using natural language.

**Sign in with your Okta account** to get started. You'll only be able to manage groups that your administrator has assigned to you.

**Example requests:**
- "Add jane.doe@company.com to the Engineering group"
- "Who is currently in the Sales team?"
- "Look up the user john.smith@company.com"
